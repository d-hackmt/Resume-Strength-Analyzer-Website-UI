Resume Strength Analyzer Chatbot

Welcome to our Resume Strength Analyzer chatbot. Our chatbot is designed to help you improve your resume by providing you with recommendations on which skills to include based on the job requirements. Our chatbot is trained on our data for the Resume Strength Analyzer project, which uses NLP and deep learning techniques to predict the probability of getting placed, with a focus on tech roles.
