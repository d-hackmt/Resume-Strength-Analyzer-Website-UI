Welcome to Resume Checker

Our system analyzes resume content and predicts the probability of getting placed. We help bridge the gap between the company's expectations and the user's capabilities by recommending which skill sets are most important according to the job role. Our system also helps categorize skill sets and highlight them in the resume.

How It Works

Our algorithm uses machine learning to analyze your resume and compare it to job postings to determine which skills and experiences are most relevant. Based on this analysis, we provide personalized recommendations for improving your resume.

Why Choose Us

Our system is easy to use and provides valuable insights to help you improve your chances of getting hired. Our team of experts is constantly updating our algorithm to ensure that we provide the most accurate and up-to-date recommendations.
